Software Resources

We utilize a comprehensive suite of state-of-the-art computational chemistry packages and material modeling software:

  • Gaussian & Linda: For electronic structure modeling and parallel execution across network clusters.
  • VASP (Vienna Ab initio Simulation Package): For atomic-scale materials modeling from first principles.
  • Molpro: For high-accuracy quantum chemistry molecular electronic structure calculations.
  • Materials Studio & DMol3: For simulating and predicting the properties of molecules, crystals, and 2D materials.
